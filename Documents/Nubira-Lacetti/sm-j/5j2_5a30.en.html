Disassembly Procedure

  1. Using a suitable puller, remove the tapered roller bearing from the transaxle case side.



  2. Using a press, remove the counter driven gear.



  3. Using a suitable puller, remove the tapered roller bearing.
  4. Using a suitable puller, remove the outer tapered roller bearing race and shim from the transaxle case.
  5. Using a suitable puller, remove the outer tapered roller bearing race from the transaxle housing.



Assembly Procedure

  1. Install a new outer tapered roller bearing race into the transaxle housing.

    2. Notice : Press-fit the outer tapered roller bearing race until it contacts the transaxle housing.




  2. Install the shim to the transaxle case.
    • First install a shim of the same thickness as before.
  3. Using transaxle case outer tapered roller bearing race adapter DW240-160 and adapter handle DW240-170, press in a new outer tapered roller bearing race into the transaxle case.

    4. Notice : Press-fit the shim until it contacts the transaxle case.




  4. Using a press, install the counter driven gear.



  5. Using a press, install a new tapered roller bearing of the transaxle case side.



  6. Using a press, install a new tapered roller bearing of .the transaxle housing side.

Disassembly Procedure

  1. Write down the relative position of the primary regulator valve sleeve, plug and pin.

    2. Notice : Because the line pressure is changed by the position of the pin and groove in the sleeve end, be sure to check the position.

  2. Remove the manual valve.



  3. Remove the 5 bolts and 5 shift solenoid valves.



  4. Remove the 15 bolts.



  5. Remove the cover, 2 gaskets and plate.



  6. Remove the spring, check valve and check ball.
  7. Remove the bolt, spring seat, spring and ball.
  8. Turn over the valve body.



  9. Remove the 2 bolts from the valve body.



  10. Remove the upper valve body from the lower valve body with plate.



  11. Remove the valve body plate from the lower valve body.

    12. Notice : Be careful that the check balls do not fall out.




Assembly Procedure

  1. Install the valve body plate to the lower valve body.



  2. Install the upper valve body to the lower valve body with plate.



  3. Install the 2 bolts to the valve body.

    4. Tighten
    Tighten the bolts to 11 N•m (97 lb-in).

    Bolt A: 49 mm (1.93 in.)
    Bolt B: 36 mm (1.42 in.)
  4. Turn the valve body.



  5. Install the check valve, spring and check ball.
  6. Install the ball, spring, spring seat and bolt.

    7. Tighten
    Tighten the bolts to 6.5 N•m (57 lb-in).




  7. Install the 2 gaskets, plate and lower valve body cover.



  8. First, tighten the bolts indicated by arrows temporarily and then tighten the other bolts temporarily.
  9. Tighten all the bolts completely.

    10. Tighten
    Tighten the bolts to 11 N•m (97 lb-in).

      • Bolt length:
      Bolt A: 49 mm (1.93 in.)
      Bolt B: 20 mm (0.79 in.)
      Bolt C: 60 mm (2.36 in.)



  10. Install the 5 shift solenoid valves with the 6 bolts.

    11. Tighten
    Tighten the bolts to 11 N•m (97 lb-in).

    • Bolt length:
    Bolt A: 49 mm (1.93 in.)
    Bolt B: 20 mm (0.79 in.)
  11. Install the manual valve.



  12. Make sure that the primary regulator valve is positioned in the same place where it was removed.

Disassembly Procedure

  1. Place the matchmarks on the ring gear and differential case.



  2. Remove the 8 bolts.



  3. Using a plastic hammer, tap on the ring gear to remove it from the case.



  4. Using a suitable puller, remove the tapered roller bearing of transaxle housing side.



  5. Using a suitable puller, remove the tapered roller bearing of transaxle case side.



  6. Using a pin punch and hammer, drive out the pin from the ring gear side.



  7. Remove the differential pinion shaft from the case.



  8. Remove the 2 differential pinion gears, 2 differential side gears, 2 differential pinion thrust washers and 2 differential side gear thrust washers.



  9. Using a driver, remove the oil seal from the transaxle housing.

    10. Notice : Be careful not to damage the transaxle housing when removing the oil seal using a driver.




  10. Using a suitable puller, remove the side bearing outer race of transaxle housing.



  11. Using a driver, remove the oil seal of transaxle case.

    12. Notice : Be careful not to damage the transaxle case when removing the oil seal using a driver.




  12. Using transaxle case side bearing outer race adapter DW240-150 and adapter handle DW240-170, remove the transaxle case side bearing outer race and shim.



Assembly Procedure

  1. Using transaxle housing side bearing outer race adapter DW240-140 and adapter handle DW240-170, install a new side bearing outer race into the transaxle housing.



  2. Install the shim to the transaxle case.

    3. Notice : First install a shim of the same thickness as before.

  3. Install a new side bearing outer race into the transaxle case.



  4. Coat the thrust washers, side gears, pinion gears and pinion thrust washers with ATF.
  5. Install the 2 differential side gear thrust washers to 2 differential side gears.



  6. Install the 2 side gears with 2 thrust washers, 2 pinion gears and 2 pinion thrust washers into the differential case.
  7. Coat the pinion shaft with ATF.



  8. Install the pinion shaft so as to align the lock pin holes on the pinion shaft and differential case.



  9. Measure the side gear backlash while holding 1 pinion gear toward the case.
    • Standard backlash:
    0.05 – 0.20 mm (0.0020-0.0079 in.)
    If the backlash is out of specification, install the correct thrust washer to the side gear.
  10. Referring to the table below, select thrust washers which will ensure that the backlash is within the specification.
    11. Try to select washers of the same size for both sides.

  11. Using a hammer and punch, drive in the pin through the case and hole in the pinion shaft.



  12. Using a hammer and chisel, stake the differential case.



  13. Using a press, install the tapered roller bearing into the differential case.



  14. Using a press, install the tapered roller bearing into the differential case.
  15. Remove any formed in place gasket (FIPG) material and be careful not to get oil on the contacting surfaces of the transaxle housing or transaxle case.



  16. Install the differential to the transaxle case.



  17. Install the transaxle housing and 16 bolts of the transaxle housing temporarily.
  18. Tighten 8 or 9 bolts out of 16 bolts of the transaxle housing completely.

    19. Tighten
    Tighten the bolts to 29 N•m (22 lb-ft).




  19. Using differential preload adapter DW240-130, turn the differential gear assembly right and left 2 or 3 times to allow the bearing settle.

  21. Using a plastic hammer, remove the 16 bolts and transaxle housing.



  22. Remove the differential gear from the transaxle case.



  23. Clean the contact surface of the differential case.
  24. Heat the ring gear in water and boil for 10 minutes.
  25. Carefully remove the ring gear from the water.
  26. After the moisture on the ring gear has completely evaporated, quickly install the ring gear to the differential case.



  27. Install the 8 bolts.

    28. Tighten
    Tighten the bolts to 102 N•m (75 lb-ft).




  28. Using transaxle housing oil seal installer DW260-031-01 and a hammer, drive in a new transaxle housing oil seal .
    • Oil seal in depth : 3.1 mm (0.122 in.)

    • Oil seal in depth : 4.3 mm (0.169 in.)
