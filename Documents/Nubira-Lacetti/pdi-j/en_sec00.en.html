FOREWORD
This pre-delivery inspection manual describes the inspection items and procedures required before delivery of vehicle to the customer.
The descriptions and service procedures contained in this manual are based on design and method studies for the TACUMA/REZZO up to May, 2000.
Options and trims including vehicle specifications described in this manual can be changed without notice, please refer to the latest product information on option and trim availability.
Changes in design, service procedures, etc., judged to have a significant bearing on this manual, will subsequently be issued in service bulletins.
