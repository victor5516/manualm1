Diagnostic Trouble Code (DTC) P0727

Engine Speed Input Circuit No Signal

Conditions for Setting the DTC

  • TCM detects that malfunction of engine speed signal for 2 seconds.
  • No DTCs U2105

Action Taken When the DTC Sets

  • TCM will request the illumination of MIL and store DTC when TCM detects a failure on the first ignition cycle.
  • No engine torque reduction control
  • No lock-up
  • No engagement pressure control
  • No 4th gear
  • No self-learning control
  • No timing solenoid control for N-D
  • TCM judges the engine revolution is 0 rpm.

Conditions for Clearing the DTC

  • The TCM turns off the MIL when no further failures detected for three consecutive ignition cycles.
  • The scan tool can clear the DTC from the TCM history.
  • The TCM clears the DTC from the TCM history memory after forty consecutive ignition cycles without fault.

Cause of Failure

  • Wiring harness or connector between ECM and TCM
  • ECM
  • TCM

Diagnostic Trouble Code (DTC) P0741

Torque Converter Clutch (TCC) Circuit Stuck Off

Conditions for Setting the DTC

  • Lockup solenoid is stuck OFF.
  • D range selected
  • Engine water normal temperature
  • 20 seconds have passed since the transaxle was placed in D.
  • Engine revolution is 600 rpm or greater.
  • No failure of input speed sensor / engine revolution / water temperature signal / solenoid electrical / ranger sensor / throttle signal

Action Taken When the DTC Sets

  • TCM will request the illumination of MIL and store DTC when TCM detects failures on two consecutive ignition cycles.

Conditions for Clearing the DTC

  • The TCM turns off the MIL when no further failures detected for three consecutive ignition cycles.
  • The scan tool can clear the DTC from the TCM history.
  • The TCM clears the DTC from the TCM history memory after forty consecutive ignition cycles without fault.

Cause of Failure

  • Lockup solenoid
  • Inside A/T
  • Inside valve body
  • TCM

Diagnostic Trouble Code (DTC) P0743

Torque Converter Clutch (TCC) Electrical

Conditions for Setting the DTC

  • TCM detects that the ON signal of the lockup solenoid monitor during 0.5 seconds when lockup solenoid driver outputs the OFF signal.(When the TCC circuit is open or short to battery).
  • The above detection 2 times at shifting continuously.(When the Tcc circuit is open or short to battery).
  • TCM detects that the OFF signal of the lockup solenoid monitor during 0.3 seconds when lockup solenoid driver outputs the ON signal.(When the TCC circuit is short to ground).
  • The above detection 1 time at shifting continuously.(When the TCC circuit is short to ground).

Action Taken When the DTC Sets

  • TCM will request the illumination of MIL and store DTC when TCM detects failures on two consecutive ignition cycles.
  • No TCC lockup solenoid
  • 1st gear fixed under the 10km/h

Conditions for Clearing the DTC

  • The TCM turns off the MIL when no further failures detected for three consecutive ignition cycles.
  • The scan tool can clear the DTC from the TCM history.
  • The TCM clears the DTC from the TCM history memory after forty consecutive ignition cycles without fault.
  • TCM detects the ON signal of the lockup solenoid monitor during 160 ms when lockup solenoid driver outputs the ON signal and TCM detects the OFF signal of the lockup solenoid monitor during 160 ms when lockup solenoid driver outputs the OFF signal.

Cause of Failure

  • Wiring harness or connector between lockup solenoid and TCM
  • Lockup solenoid
  • TCM

Diagnostic Trouble Code (DTC) P0748

Pressure Control Solenoid (PCS) Electrical

Conditions for Setting the DTC

  • TCM detects that the feed back voltage is greater than 4.9 volts and detects the detection 500 milliseconds continuously.(When the PCS circuit is short to battery).
  • TCM detects that the feed back voltage is less than 70.mV for 70 milliseconds and detects the 12.5 seconds continuously.(When the PCS circuit is short to ground or open).

Action Taken When the DTC Sets

  • TCM will request the illumination of MIL and store DTC when TCM detects a failure on the first ignition cycle.
  • No lockup control
  • No engine torque reduction control
  • No engagement pressure control
  • No 4th gear
  • No self-learning control
  • After failure decision : emergency mode

Conditions for Clearing the DTC

  • The TCM turns off the MIL when no further failures detected for three consecutive ignition cycles.
  • The scan tool can clear the DTC from the TCM history.
  • The TCM clears the DTC from the TCM history memory after forty consecutive ignition cycles without fault.
  • TCM detects the feed back voltage is between 70mV and 4.9 v for 12.5 seconds continuously.

Cause of Failure

  • Wiring harness or connector between Pressure Control Solenoid (PCS) and TCM
  • PCS
  • TCM
